Born in 1959, in the mountians of Kirkuk, Kurdistan. Zana Zangana's placment on this earth was in a region known for it's rich history of our past in philosophy, educational, resources, and perhaps most notably religion. He was raised under the dark shadow of a opressive government known for genocide and ethnocide, that had claimed as a authoritive messager of God. This along with ohter grim events of injustice that had been erroneously declared rightous by the word of God, began a chain events within Zana to question the faith that was etched into his daily life.

After many hard years of cultural persecution, to which he would see his father inprisoned several times and lose beloved family members. Zana, departed the land so close to his heart in persuite of a better life. These travels took him to various countries of Europe, and the begining of his research for the questions he had of mankind's relationship with God. Now, equipped with resources once not available in his native country, he set out to find clues to why the message of a Creator of such splender could bare the depravity of such actsions of our past. Spending countless hours exploring the literaure works of greatest minds of our past to attempt to connect a path to how or where a message from a creative force could be so deceived.

After many many years in Europe and starting a family, his journey took him to the other side of the world to the United States in Ann Arbor, MI. He attended Eastern Michigan University, in which he persued his studies in philosphy and religion along with other areas of education including economics. When faced with the desicion to persue a masters in economics or his passion of philosphy and theology, he chose a path that at the time was one that he believed would provide a better finacial situatino for his family of three growing kids. Though urged by a proffesor of Theolgy from the University of Michigan, which they had many of discussions on his research, to change his mind. Zana, ultimarlty put his dream and passion on hold to provide a better life for his family. However, this would not put on hold his desire to search for evidence of a answer to his questions.

After, graduating from Eastern Michigan Univeristy with a masters in economics, he regonized a gaping problem involving the many international communities in the area and the lack of offordiable food from thier respected countries. Witnessing larger grocery stores or specialized smaller stores offering these internatioanl groceries at a steep price. Understanding and reminiscent of his own experience of being in new country. Zana, started a grocery buisness that not ony would offer a wider selection of grocery and produce from various countries but also at a offordable price. His passion to help the international community of various countries feel more comfortable in a new land as made his buisness a stable in the community for over twenty years. A success that produced a second store in a community one ohur north of Ann Arbor with a large and diverse community of internatianal families.

WHile his many years of hard work to build his buisness was a staple in his life, the passion of his search for the questions of his youth never wandered. Building a library of literature over the many years. He even created a study area in the back of his stores in order to research when ever he had a chance.

Currently, he is writing new book the wraps a fictional story line with real events of his life growing up in Kurdistan.
